#peatfree

What do you want us to do?

Ban the use of peat in gardening and horticulture

Background

Buying peat-based compost contributes to the destruction of wildlife habitats by peat mining & the release of vast amounts of carbon dioxide, the main agent of climate breakdown. To tackle climate breakdown & its impact on our natural world, we need to ban the use of peat in gardening & horticulture

Additional details

  • 3 million cubic metres of peat sold for horticultural use annually in UK, ⅓ from UK peatlands (IUCN)
  • 10 million tonnes carbon dioxide lost to atmosphere annually from UK’s damaged peatlands (IUCN)
  • Peatland destruction releasing vast amounts of CO2 (New Scientist)
  • Peatlands support many important species & unique ecosystems (IUCN)
